Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining.

Do I need a real estate license?
Yes, you must hold an active real estate license in Virginia and/or Maryland. We verify all licenses before activation.
How are leads distributed?
Leads are matched to agents based on ZIP code zones. When a new lead comes in, you receive an SMS with the client's details. You can accept or pass on any lead.
What's the commission split?
You keep 75% of the total commission (based on 3% of sale price). DLCR retains 25% as the referral fee. Commission is paid at closing.
Are there any fees to join?
No. There are no desk fees, monthly fees, or startup costs. You only share commission when a deal successfully closes.
What areas do you cover?
We generate leads across Northern Virginia (Fairfax, Arlington, Alexandria, Loudoun, Prince William) and Maryland (Montgomery County, Bethesda, Rockville, Silver Spring, Prince George's County).
Can I work with my own clients too?
Absolutely. The referral network is supplemental. You keep 100% of any deals you bring independently. Our leads are additional business on top of your existing pipeline.
How quickly will I get leads?
Once activated, you'll start receiving leads as they come in for your zones. Volume depends on market activity in your area, but most agents receive their first lead within the first week.
